PC Overview


We aimed to create the best gaming PC under $1,000 with this setup! At this price range, you don't have to compromise much on performance. You're now in the higher end of mid-range PCs, where playing AAA games at 1080p is easy, and you can even handle 1440p gaming well.

What's cool about this build is that the graphics card (GPU) and storage will stay relevant for a long time. The CPU and motherboard are solid too, but they're from the last generation. You might consider upgrading them first in the future. Overall, we think this is one of the top choices for a gaming PC around the $1,000 mark. Keep in mind that hardware prices can change daily, but our team works hard to keep the total cost close to $1,000.

What you get with your $1000


When building a gaming PC at this price point, it takes some extra consideration to ensure you get the best experience possible. However, even with careful planning, you can still achieve a high-end gaming setup for $1000.

The Nvidia RTX 4070 graphics card is a standout feature in this price range, offering advanced capabilities like ray tracing and Nvidia's DLSS 3 technology. With this card, you can expect exceptional gaming performance across various resolutions.

While you may not be able to afford the absolute top-of-the-line graphics cards at this budget, the mid-range options available today are more than sufficient in terms of power and performance for most gaming needs.

Performance


You have the option to choose between AMD or Intel CPUs for this build, but AMD's 5000-series CPUs still offer excellent performance for the price. When it comes to graphics, expect outstanding results at 1080p and 1440p, even on max settings or with a high refresh rate monitor. You might need to adjust settings in some cases, but you can even do some gaming in 4K, although we recommend this build for those with a 1440p monitor or lower.

This build includes a 1TB NVMe SSD, providing ample space for your operating system and games. Not only does this SSD offer faster boot times, but it also enhances gaming performance. Speaking of performance, let's not forget about the 16GB RAM kit included in this build. While it operates at a clock speed of 3200MHz, it still boosts your gaming performance and remains reliable for years to come.
