Our chosen CPU is AMD's Ryzen 9 7950X3D, renowned for its superior gaming performance compared to any Intel CPU currently available, including the Core i9-14900K. Even the more budget-friendly Ryzen 7 7800X3D delivers excellent gaming performance, although it lacks the same number of cores for productivity tasks.
