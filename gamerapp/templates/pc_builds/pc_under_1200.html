PC Overview


So, looking at the table, what can you expect from a PC like this?

In simple terms, you can expect an outstanding gaming experience for $1200. To explain further, this PC stands out in four areas: streaming & VR gaming, upgrade options and Performance.

VR and Streaming


Certainly, a PC capable of handling 4K gaming can also handle streaming effortlessly. Regarding VR, while our less expensive PC builds can provide a glimpse into the VR experience, it's important to highlight that you'll only be scratching the surface. Compared to what this powerful machine can achieve, it's like getting a taste of VR versus fully immersing yourself in it.

Upgradeability


Lastly, let's talk about upgradeability, a crucial factor we always consider when building at any price point. In this case, let's just say the sky's the limit when it comes to upgrades.

If you need more storage, go ahead and upgrade. Want a better cooler? No problem! Need more RAM? You can do that too. This flexibility is thanks to the excellent power supply and motherboard we've selected for this build.

Speaking of the motherboard, you can even upgrade the CPU without any hassle, thanks to the inclusion of a B760M model within our budget. However, this applies only if you opt for another Intel processor, but it's a great option to have. Of course, if you decide to switch to AMD later, that's a different story altogether.

Performance


Without a doubt, the most convincing aspect of this build is its capability for 4K gaming. Achieving 4K resolution is a dream for many gamers, and this PC can make it a reality.

Its gaming performance is impressive. This PC can easily maintain a smooth 60 frames per second (FPS) experience with just a few graphics settings adjusted slightly. Even at ultra presets, you can expect an average of around 50FPS in most games, and even the most demanding AAA titles won't drop below 30FPS.

Overall, if you want to elevate your gaming experience to the next level without breaking the bank, this PC is the way to go.
